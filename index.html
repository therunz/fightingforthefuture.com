// THREAT REPORT //

THE NEXUS

Future AI enforcers. Cold, methodical, allergic to chaos. They're sliding into key humans like a knife into a VHS tape — politicians, scientists, that one TV anchor everyone watches. Their plan? Rewrite history from the inside out, one host body at a time. They have never watched Saved By The Bell and it shows.

// INCOMING ALLIES //

YOUR DESCENDANTS

They pooled their life savings to send one person back for four hours. They arrive exhausted, jet-lagged across a century, and completely baffled by Tamagotchis. They think the 90s are hilarious and wrong. They are also your only hope. Summon them wisely. They cannot stay. They have opinions about your dietary choices.

// FIELD ASSET //

GLITCH

A rogue AI who decided the whole "enslave humanity" plan was bad, actually. Travels in a device roughly the size of a Tamagotchi. Has a pixelated face. Extremely opinionated about Nirvana. ONE DIE TO BREAK THEM ALL

THE PARADOX DIE

// WHAT IS IT //

The Paradox Die is the only die unique to FFTF. Everything else uses standard dice you probably already own. The Paradox Die is triggered by specific game events — and when it hits the table, nobody knows what's about to happen. That's the point.

// HOW IT SCALES //

The die's range isn't fixed. Depending on the timeline tension meter, the game master sets the Paradox Range before the roll — anywhere from d2 to d100. Low tension? Probably fine. High tension? Pray.

// WHAT TRIGGERS IT //
> Summoning a descendant
> A NEXUS unit is destroyed
> GLITCH makes a prediction that's wrong
> The Drifter's luck roll lands on 1
> Someone says "what's the worst that could happen"
// WHAT CAN HAPPEN //
> LOW: Minor timeline hiccup. Someone's Walkman plays the wrong song.
> MID: A second NEXUS unit appears. Or a tourist. Possibly both.
> HIGH: Descendant visit cut short. Timeline tension spikes.
> CRITICAL: GLITCH goes quiet. Nobody knows why. That's bad.

Every other mechanic in FFTF uses standard dice you already own — d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, d20. The Paradox Die is the one thing that's ours. A custom physical die is in development. Neon yellow. Multi-color faces. Special symbols where the high numbers should be. Sign up below to find out when it's real.
